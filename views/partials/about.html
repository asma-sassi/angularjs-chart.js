Our story :

Our goal is to redesign the way we deliver video online, to create more robust, cost-effective infrastructures, and to bring high quality video to every corner of the world. With a hybrid peer-to-peer solution to video streaming, we aim to help online broadcasters improve quality of service, scale to growing audiences and cut bandwidth costs by up to 70%.
